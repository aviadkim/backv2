Supported Documents

The Financial Document Processor supports various types of financial documents, including:

  • Portfolio Reports: Documents showing portfolio holdings, values, and allocations
  • Bank Statements: Monthly or quarterly statements from financial institutions
  • Investment Summaries: Summaries of investment accounts and performance
  • Financial Statements: Balance sheets, income statements, etc.
  • Tax Documents: Tax-related financial documents

For best results, upload PDF documents with clear text and tables.

Processing Information

After uploading, your document will be processed to extract:

  • Portfolio value
  • Securities (stocks, bonds, funds, etc.)
  • Asset allocation
  • Risk profile
  • Other financial information

Processing may take a few moments depending on the document size and complexity.
